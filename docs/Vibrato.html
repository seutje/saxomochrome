Vibrato → Tone.Effect

A Vibrato effect composed of a Tone.Delay and a Tone.LFO. The LFO modulates the delayTime of the delay, causing the pitch to rise and fall.

new Tone.Vibrato (frequency, depth)
frequency {Frequency}

The frequency of the vibrato.

depth {NormalRange}

The amount the pitch is modulated.

default values
maxDelay:0.005
frequency:5
depth:0.1
type:"sine"
